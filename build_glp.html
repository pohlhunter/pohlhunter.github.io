Build-a-GLP GLP

LLAB training plan was changed last minute, and you must come up with a GLP for Alpha and Bravo flights as soon as possible! Materials are provided, use as many as you want or none at all. You must provide directions or rules of engagement if needed, along with limits and parameters that may need to be specified, and an objective to be completed.

You have 5 minutes for planning.

20 minutes for execution, to create this GLP.

20 minutes will be given to the POC flight to complete your GLP. You may set planning and execution times.
