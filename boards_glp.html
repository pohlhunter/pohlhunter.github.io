Triple Board Sweep GLP

Several points of interest are placed in cells on a board. An unmanned surveillance aircraft located in the upper left cell of the board, must survey as many points as possible. And end at FOB W, at the bottom right cell.

However, on each move, the aircraft CAN ONLY move either one cell to the RIGHT OR DOWN from its current location.

You must attempt to survey as many points as possible. You must submit the best route to HQ for each board, before time is up!

Reminder: starting point and end point are marked on the board. Points of interest are marked by ‘X’
