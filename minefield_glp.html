Minefield GLP

Split into THREE teams. Each team gets one blindfold. Each team will have one blindfolded member that navigates the ‘minefield’.

Team members will have to guide their blinded member through the minefield to complete the tasks.

The tasks are to pick up as many of the objects up and return them to your team. The objects are anything that is not a mine of course.

The team with the most objects returned wins!

The mines are the pieces of paper.
