Two Blind for Air Power

Your flight has been tasked with establishing air dominance in the area. To establish air dominance, you must construct two aircraft and deploy efficiently.

The problem, however, is that during the start of the mission the pilots have become blind from a firefight.

Make two paper airplanes, and choose ONE PERSON to throw each toward the target area to establish air dominance. The ‘pilot’ will not know where the target area is exactly, so you must communicate with them effectively! The target area will be a water bottle established by HQ.

Due to the imminent danger – you do not have a lot of time! MOVE QUICK! You are the only hope. It is important to ensure speed and excellence and start making the paper airplanes quickly. Hopefully you have begun to chose the person to throw the paper airplanes. If you are reading this still, move on, time is being wasted.


Reminder: the one throwing the paper airplane is blindfolded. To make flight commander direct the 'pilot' and see if other cadets yell over flight commander.
