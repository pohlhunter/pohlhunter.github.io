Transferring of command

Transferring of command for AFROTC marching is not something that is exactly a one to one from the DNC. However, there are some parts of the process that are descriped in the DNC.

In line formation, when calling individuals out of ranks, the command is (Rank and Last Name), (pause) FRONT AND CENTER. Upon hearing his or her name, the individual assumes the position of attention. On the command FRONT AND CENTER, the individual takes one step backward (with coordinated arm swing), faces to the left or right, proceeds to the closest flank, and then proceeds to the front of the formation by the most direct route.

Next, the individual halts one pace in front of and facing the person in command, salutes, and reports as directed.
