Tarp Flip GLP

THE SCENARIO:

“You and your crew are stranded in space on board a flexible surface in which the oxygen level is significantly running low. In 17 minutes the air will be completely gone and you and your crew will perish. On the opposite side of the surface is an abundance of oxygen. You and your team must flip the surface, the tarp, onto its opposing side in order to retrieve the oxygen.

RULES OF ENGAGEMENT:

If any part of your crew or you touches outside of the flexible surface, one minute will be taken away from the 17 minutes.

Every 5 minutes I will say 5 minutes has elapsed.

Everyone gets ONE WORD to say during the GLP. You cannot give your one word to someone else. Make sure what you say is beneficial. You cannot use any form of writing. If someone says more than one word, one minute will be taken away.

There is no planning phase. I will assign a flight commander then everyone gets on the tarp and the time starts.
