Leadership Challenge Scenario

The flight will work together to figure out what they would do as a leader. For the scenario you have 10 minutes to create AT LEAST two solutions. Then you will have 5 minutes to present the solutions with pros and cons.


The scenario:

A new wing commander has just taken over and discovers that morale among the airmen is low. They are overworked, understaffed, and feeling unappreciated. You need to give the new wing commander suggestions to solve the problem at hand.


Possible solutions:

Schedule a town hall meeting where the airmen can voice their concerns and ask questions to the new commander

Increase communication with the airmen, by creating a suggestion box, or having regular meetings

Implement a recognition program for airmen who go above and beyond their duties

Increase opportunities for training and professional development

Create a mentorship program for junior airmen
