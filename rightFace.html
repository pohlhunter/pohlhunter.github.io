Right Face

Execute facing movements from a halt, at the position of attention, and in the cadence of quick time. Perform facing movements in two counts.

The commands are Right, FACE. On the command FACE, raise the right toe and left heel slightly and pivot 90 degrees to the right on the ball of the left foot and the heel of the right foot, assisted by slight pressure on the ball of the left foot. Keep legs straight, but not stiff. The upper portion of the body remains at attention. This completes count one of the movement. Next, bring the left foot smartly forward, ensuring heels are together and on line. Feet should now be forming a 45-degree angle, which means the position of attention has been resumed. This completes count two of the movement.
