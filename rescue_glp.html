Rescue Mission GLP

1st Lieutenant Tyler Blevins has crash landed in hostile territory on BR Island. Luckily you have received communication that he has retreated to a secret safe location. The problem is, you do not know where exactly! He was able to send out pieces of information hinting at where he is. To ensure his safety, he split up the information into parts.

The map is given in another language, but the information is correct. Piece together all the information then send up the intelligence package to your Headquarters. In this package, all the Letter Locations (eg: Alpha, Bravo, Charlie) must be translated into English locations and matched up with its information (not just where Lt Blevins is). Not all Letter Locations are used. Use the translation sheet to ensure accuracy. In the package, you must include where Lieutenant Blevins is exactly, and the information about that location. The Translation Sheet can be shared, shown, and talked about; in any way you want.

The information slips cannot be shown to other cadets. The information slip can only be shared verbally.
