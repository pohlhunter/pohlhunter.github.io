Secret Codes GLP

Your group's task is to match each of the nuclear munitions with its base, secret code, yield, and the aircraft used to deliver it. All team members have access to this sheet.

Each team member will receive at least one “secret code data slip”. During the activity you may discuss the information on the cards that were assigned to you, but you may not pass the slips around for others to see, in any form.



Five nuclear munitions (in no specific order):

blue bomb; green bomb; purple missile; red missile; yellow rocket

Five bases where one each of the nuclear munitions are located: Alpha AFB; Bravo AFB; Charlie AFB; Delta AFB; Echo AFB

Five secret codes to use one each of the nuclear munitions: 727253; 1799351; 4219530; 10429538; 42911786

The yields of the five nuclear munitions (not in order): 1OK; 15K; 2OK; 25K; 30K

The aircraft that delivers one each of the five nuclear munitions (not in order): B-99; FB-50; B-5; FB-12; A-25



Handout Slips

1. The yellow rocket's secret code and the green bomb's code contain the same number of digits.

2. The red missile is delivered by the A-25.

3. The green bomb is NOT delivered by the B-5 or the B-99.

4. The yield of the nuclear munitions that's delivered by the B-99 is 15K.

5. The secret code used at Charlie AFB has six digits.

6. The yellow rocket is NOT located at Delta AFB.

7. The yield of the nuclear munitions located at Alpha AFB is 15K.

8. The blue bomb and the purple missile are NOT located at Charlie AFB or Delta AFB.

9. The nuclear munitions’ secret code delivered by the B-99 is 42911786.

10. The yield of the nuclear munitions with the secret code 4219530 is 20K.

11. The yield of the nuclear munitions that's delivered by the B-5 is less than 15K.

12. The yellow rocket is delivered by the FB-12.

13. The yield of the nuclear munitions located at Charlie AFB is more than 10K.

14. The yield of the green bomb is 25K.

15. The FB-12 is in an underground hanger at Echo AFB.

16. The blue bomb's secret code is 10429538.

17. The green bomb’s secret code starts with a “1” and is NOT at Bravo AFB.

18. The purple missile is at Alpha AFB.
